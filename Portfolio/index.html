I am Corné Napier, a Software Developer

Since I can remember, I've always been fascinated with tech. From taking apart my xbox controller and learning how to mod it, fixing a broken pc, learning how to add mods to games I loved, to creating my own web browser and text based game in C++.

I studied psychology at university to get a better understanding of myself and human nature. I've always enjoyed figuring out how things work, social interactions, impacts of body language, how the brain communicates with the rest of the body. I guess you could say I was always interested in how the back-end processes work in conjunction with the front-end.

I used to be envious of people who had a gift for creativity in the arts, becuase I was only capable of drawing stickmen and my singing is far from great. However, after writting my first line of code and bringing together a small project, I came to realise that in programming/coding I too can be creative and artistic.

I enjoy what I do, because I am someone who loves a challenge and to learn new information, and in Software Development I have found that these factors are in abundance.
